A bit of history

How it started

Back in 1998, when Funko was founded by Mike and Claudia Becker, they started making bobblehead figures. The idea of starting the company came to mike after failing to find an affordable coin bank of the Big Boy Restaurants mascot. At the time, Funko was only producing bobblehead figures.

How it went

It's only in 2010, after Brian Mariotti who took over Funko in 2005, that the first Funko Pop! was created. The success of Funko became so huge, they had to find new headquarters. In 2017, the office moved from Lynnwood to Everett. From there, Funko even became listed on the NASDAQ stock exchange.

How it's going

Nowadays, Funko counts different lines of products besides Pops. They have also aquired the fashion accessory line Loungefly and the high-end pop culture company Mondo. Funko is competing with the other toys giants such as Hasbro and Mattel.
